BlackJack Count Trainer

Click through the deck at your own pace with "Next Card" or have the cards dealt automatically and at the speed of your choice by clicking either "Auto Deal- Slow" "Auto Deal- Med" or "Auto Deal- Fast".
The cards will shuffle and reset each time they hit zero. If you followed the count correctly it should be at zero. At any time you can take a peek at the current count by hovering your mouse over "Count:"
